Gallivanter Baller

The Gallivanter Baller is the third-generation Baller model from Malysia car maker HX and was originally developed under the codename 'L30'.

Specifications and performance

The Gallivanter Baller's exterior was updated for 2006 along with the Benefactor V8 being replaced with a Felon unit. The new engine choices were Felon's AJ-V8, with 4.4-litre 300 hp (220 kW) or 4.2-litre 400 hp (300 kW) supercharged variants. This new Gallivanter Baller was officially presented at the 2019 Malaysia International Auto Show and released in summer 2019.
