Bravado Rumpo

The Bravado Rumpo is a range of full-size vans produced by the Malysia automaker HX since 2019.

Specifications and performance

The Rumpo performs much better than the standard model, since the off-road tires, combined with its high suspension, makes it much easier to drive over uneven terrains. the vehicle is powered by a single-cam V8 engine, coupled to a 5-gearbox in a front engine, all wheel drive layout. Its engine sound is also unique, being a higher-pitched and louder variation of the van sound commonly found on vans like the Speedo or the Youga.
