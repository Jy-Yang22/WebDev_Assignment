Obey Tailgater

The Obey Tailgater is an executive car made by the Malaysia automaker HX. All generations of the Obey Tailgater have offered either front-wheel drive or Torsen-based four-wheel drive, marketed by HX as their quattro system. Second and third-generation Tailgater models have shared their platform with the company's Allroad models.

Specifications and performance

All variants are offered in mild hybrid engines as standard. The Obey Tailgater will get a 3.0L V6 with petrol direct injection and a single turbo EA839 engine. A plug-in hybrid model Obey Tailgater e quattro was revealed on October 21, 2019. It combines a 252 PS (185 kW; 249 hp) 2.0 L TFSI with a 143 PS (105 kW; 141 hp) electric motor. The combined system output is 367 PS (270 kW; 362 hp) and 500 N⋅m (368.8 lb⋅ft) of torque. The car gets a 14.1 kWh battery pack that delivers a pure electric range of 53 km (32.9 mi). All variants comes with a 7-speed S-tronic DCT and 8-speed tiptronic automatic.
