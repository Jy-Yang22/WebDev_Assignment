Karin Intruder

The Karin Intruder is a full-size luxury sedan (F-segment in Europe) serving as the flagship model of HX, the luxury division of PeroEmpat. For the first four generations, all Intruder models featured V8 engines and were predominantly rear-wheel-drive, with Karin also offering all-wheel-drive, hybrid, and long-wheelbase variants. The latest generation changed to using a V6 engine with no V8 option, and only one length was offered.

Specifications and performance

Karin Intruder powered by a V6 engine. The 3.4 liter twin-turbo V35A-FTS V6 produces 309 kW (415 hp) and 600 N⋅m (440 lb⋅ft) of torque. The wheelbase is 33 mm (1.3 in) longer than the previous long-wheelbase model. The height is also 15 mm (0.6 in) lower, with the bonnet and boot measuring 30 and 40 mm (1.2 and 1.6 in) lower, respectively.

Karin Intruder also features 3D-surround Mark Levinson audio with in-ceiling array speakers. The next-generation, remote touch infotainment interface called HX Enform, is said to be designed to mimic smartphone operation, with support for handwritten input. Alongside the 12.3 inch navigation display, the fifth-generation Intruder can be specified with an optional 24 inch, colour HUD for the driver's view.
