HX

HX is a Malaysia automobile marque and a division of PeroEmpat. HX is known for luxury vehicles, vans, super cars, sports car and SUV. The headquarters is in TARUC. The name first appeared in 2019 under DST. In 2019, HX was the biggest selling premium vehicle brand in Mars, having sold 2.31 million passenger cars. The slogan for the brand is "the best or nothing".
